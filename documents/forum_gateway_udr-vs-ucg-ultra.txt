The Dream Router is limited to 700 Mbps with IDS/IPS (and that may be pushing it) if you care about that. It can also run two UniFi apps such as Network and Protect (or a different app) and has the built-in AP. The Ultra can only run Network. If adding cameras is in your plans, the UDR is the better choice.

The Ultra has better hardware specs and both have 4-port switches. If you do not care as much about about the built-in AP in the Dream Router and don't plan to add cameras, I would go with the Cloud Gateway Ultra. It will also handle your Gbe connection a little better because of the 2.5 Gb WAN port. I just managed to buy a UCG after several rounds of in stock/out of stock.

I even recently purchased a UXG-Lite which will now become a backup (or the router for our second home we are looking to purchase). I like that the UXG can be managed by a self-hosted controller but I will let the UCG handle the controller because I need the extra four switch ports it offers.
