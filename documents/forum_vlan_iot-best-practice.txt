I've been reading quite a few blog posts now to make this work. Then I found that Ubiquity has an excellent help page that made me rewrite my whole setup. The below works for me

Step 1. Follow option 1 and 3 in https://help.ui.com/hc/en-us/articles/115010254227

note the clear-connection-tracking tip at the bottom of the page, very helpful while testing!!
Step 2. Open IoT to use my Plex Media Server: Add firewall rule allowing IoT network to address/port for my Plex Media Server

Step 3. Enable mDns (in "Settings -> Gateway -> mDns) (for Chromecast)

(I've have also disabled IGMP Snooping all over, but I'm not sure if that's necessary?)
