In that case, then it's a great alternative. Although keep in mind that the UCG-Ultra has only a 1gbps pipe between the switch chip and the CPU, and the switch ports are all 1gbps. So if you have a heavy WiFi environment or heavy inter-vlan traffic, then that's a reason to go with the UDMP with a Pro or higher PoE switch.
